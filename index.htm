Hiker Paradise

A wilderness movement calling believers to repentance, prayer, self-reliance, and the formation of a mountain refuge community.

The Gospel Call

Return to repentance, obedience to Jesus Christ, prayer, and true discipleship.

The Movement

A growing fellowship of believers seeking simplicity, truth, and separation from worldly systems.

Self-Reliance

Food production, wilderness skills, preparedness, and mutual support among believers.

The Mountain Vision

The goal is to establish a physical mountain headquarters where believers can gather in tents, pray, work, and build a shared life centered on scripture, prayer, and simplicity.
